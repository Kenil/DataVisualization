For the purpose of this study, Accuracy is defined as least amount of errors, highest amount of aces, and higher percentage of first serve. It is observed that player who excels in all three attributes (or is most accurate) is guaranteed to win.

Attribute Definition
First Serve The first of the two attempts to serve that a player is allowed at the beginning of a point. Success rate of first serve is measured in percentage.
Aces Legal serve that is not touched by the receiver, winning the point
Errors Error in a service or return shot that cannot be attributed to any factor other than poor judgement and execution by the player

NOTE:There may exist more trends in the data and those are left for readers to find.
